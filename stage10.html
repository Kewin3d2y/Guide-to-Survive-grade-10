You followed a trail across ten rooms — a crime scene, a library, a chemistry lab, an art room, a music room, a rooftop, an old classroom, an infirmary, a principal's office, and finally, this garden.

Ten clues. Ten puzzles. Each one asking you to be curious, to think, to not give up when it got hard. Which is — not coincidentally — exactly what Grade 10 will ask of you, every single day.

That person is going to be just fine.
